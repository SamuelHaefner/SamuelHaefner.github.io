Teaching

I have over 10 years of teaching experience. Below is a list of courses that I have taught.

Contract Theory (in English). M.Sc. elective.

  • Place and dates: University of Basel. Spring terms 2014, 2015, 2016, 2018 (except for 2014, Georg Nöldeke and I each taught half of the course).

  • Content: Canonical models of moral hazard and adverse selection.

  • Textbook: Patrick Bolton and Mathias Dewatripont, Contract Theory, MIT Press, 2004.

Introduction to Game Theory (in English). B.Sc.

  • Place and dates: University of Zurich. Fall terms 2017, 2018.

  • Content: Static and dynamic games as well as games of complete and incomplete information.

  • Textbook: Steven Tadelis, Game Theory. An Introduction, Princeton UP, 2013.

Contest Theory (Ökonomische Theorie der Wettkämpfe, in German). M.Sc. elective.

  • Place and dates: University of Basel. Fall terms 2011, 2012, 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018.

  • Content: Tullock Contest, All-Pay Auction, and Applications.

  • Textbook: Kai Konrad, Strategy and Dynamics in Contests, Oxford UP, 2009.

Insurance Economics (Versicherungsökonomie, in German). B.Sc.

  • Place and dates: University of Basel. Spring terms 2015, 2016, 2018, 2019 (except for 2019, Georg Nöldeke and I each taught half of the course).

  • Content: Microeconomics of insurance markets, including expected utility, demand for insurance.

  • Textbook: Hugh Gravelle und Ray Rees, Microeconomics, Pearson Education, 2007 (Chapters 17 and 19).

Microeconomics II (Mikroökonomie II, in German, tutoring only). B.Sc.

  • Place and date: University of St. Gallen. Fall term 2020.

  • Content: Intermediate microeconomics.

  • Textbook: A. Goolsbee, S. Levitt, und C. Syverson, Microeconomics, Macmillan, 2020.
